# Playbook: weekly-debrief

**Trigger:** "run the weekly-debrief playbook"

**What it does:**
1. Read every note in all Feedback folders updated in the last 7 days.
2. Read all Outputs from this week across active ventures.
3. Pull my north star metric from CLAUDE.md and check if any Feedback notes updated it.
4. Write a dated founder debrief to `Outputs/weekly-debriefs/` covering:
   - What shipped this week (be specific — features, deals, hires, content)
   - What stalled and the honest reason why
   - Key signals captured (competitor moves, customer insights, market shifts)
   - North star metric: current value vs. target
   - The one decision I need to make next week that I've been avoiding
5. Link the debrief to the relevant venture pages and competitor notes.
6. Surface one pattern across the last 4 weeks of debriefs that I probably haven't named yet.

**Notes:**
- Keep it under 500 words. Clarity over completeness.
- Be direct about what stalled. Do not soften it.
- The pattern in step 6 is the most valuable part. Take the time.
