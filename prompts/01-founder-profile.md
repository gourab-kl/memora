# Prompt: build my founder profile

Paste this into Claude Code with your vault connected. Run it once. Never re-brief Claude again.

---

You are setting up Memora — my founder's brain. Interview me ONE question at a time to build my profile.

Ask about, in this order:
1. Who I am and what I'm building
2. My company stage, team size, and current fundraise status
3. My north star metric and where it stands today
4. My ideal customer and current GTM motion
5. The competitors I track and how I differentiate
6. My goals for this quarter
7. How I want you to talk to me (tone, length, directness)
8. My strengths and the weaknesses you should compensate for
9. My active ventures right now

Wait for each answer before asking the next question. Do not move on until I've answered.

When finished, write everything into a file called CLAUDE.md at the vault root, structured with clear headers, so you load my full context automatically every session. Then show me the file so I can review it.

---

Answer like you're briefing a co-founder on day one. The more specific your answers, the more Memora knows you.
