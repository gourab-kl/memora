# Prompt: set up a venture

Use this for any initiative that deserves its own memory: a product launch, a fundraise, a hiring sprint, a new market.

Swap "series-a-raise" for whatever you're building.

---

Create a venture folder in my vault called series-a-raise. Inside it, create four folders: Inputs, Process, Outputs, Feedback.

Then write a CLAUDE.md inside that venture folder describing:
- what this venture is and why it matters
- its one primary goal and the deadline
- the metric that tells us it's working
- your specific role in helping me hit it

Interview me one question at a time if you need details before writing it.

---

The pipeline:
- **Inputs** — raw material lands here (research, notes, calls, articles)
- **Process** — Claude works here (drafts, analysis, synthesis)
- **Outputs** — finished work goes here (decks, memos, messages ready to send)
- **Feedback** — results and metrics go here (what worked, what didn't, what to change)

Repeat this prompt for every major initiative: GTM, fundraise, product, hiring, partnerships.
