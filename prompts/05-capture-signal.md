# Prompt: capture a market signal

Use this when you drop a competitor teardown, investor memo, market report, podcast transcript, or customer call note into an Inputs folder. Claude files it, links it, and surfaces what matters for your specific position.

---

Read the new file(s) in my Inputs folder. For each one:
1. Write or update a summary page in your own words (300–600 words).
2. Link it to every related page already in the vault using [[wikilinks]] — competitor pages, ICP notes, GTM strategy, active ventures.
3. Update the index.
4. Note the source at the bottom of each page so every claim is traceable.
5. Flag anything in this source that changes how I should think about my ICP, my positioning, or my north star metric.

If this source contradicts something already in the vault, do not overwrite. Flag both claims with their sources and tell me.

When done, give me a one-line note of which pages you created or touched, and one insight worth acting on this week.

---

**Best used for:**
- Competitor product updates or pricing changes
- Customer call transcripts and discovery notes
- VC memos, market maps, or industry reports
- Podcast or conference talks from domain experts
- Inbound signals: sales objections, churn reasons, NPS comments
